About

The purpose of Mouser is to move the mouse automatically every minute. It only moves the mouse by 2 pixels so you'll never notice it.

Why on the world would you ever need something like this? Its primary purpose is to keep the screensaver from starting. Sometimes, you are unable to change your screensaver/locking preferences and you would like to prevent them from taking effect. Mouser does this for you.
